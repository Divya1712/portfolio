About Zecruit

We, at Zecruit Facilities, offer steadfast facility management services to business firms across a wide plethora of industries. Over the years, we have collaborated with various sectors, including refinery, offshore rig platforms, oil and gas and hospitality. Backed by our seasoned pool of experts and professional expertise, we have been continuously venturing into broader sectors, catering to a diversified range of services for our clients. We have been able to carve a specialized niche in the local market, and seek to fulfil the business requirements of our clients.

Our clientele has been expanding over the years, ranging from builders and developers to educational and financial service providers. Besides, we have worked closely with several emerging IT companies, hotels, manufacturing firms, service-oriented companies and corporate firms. We evaluate the requirements of each of our clients individually while providing our facility management services. These companies, requiring a professional backup in their commercial corporate sites largely depend on us.

As an established staff contracting company, we have come a long way, after we started off our business in UAE and Europe. Particularly, we have focussed on oil and gas industries where manpower plays the pivotal role. The experience that we have picked up along the way over these years is an asset to our clients. Business firms from this sector can collaborate with us for their staffing requirements. We operate on the basis of various modules, catering to the specific needs of our clients. Working as a reliable partner with our clients, we have established our identity through consistency, dedication and professionalism. You may approach us for customized facility management services, and we would be happy to collaborate with you.

Whether you are an established firm or a start-up, recruiting the right workforce remains the key to your success. You may need temporary or permanent staffing requirements. Besides, you may also need staffing on a contractual basis. We have extensively collaborated with companies, dealing with project staffing as per their requirements.

In the competitive business environment, we acknowledge the value of time and quality. Customer satisfaction being our core priority, we make all necessary arrangements to foster a perfect operational mechanism for various businesses. Filtering the right candidate to match the desired profile and skills calls for staff contracting skills and this is where we excel. Feel free to have a consultation with us regarding your specific manpower requirements, and we would be glad to assist you.
