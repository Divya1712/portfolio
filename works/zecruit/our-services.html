Our Services

As a rapidly expanding facility management service provider, we have been pro-active in exploring newer avenues over these years. Collaborating with our clients for planning and staffing services, we operate as per the requirements of our clients. We understand the unique needs of each industry and come up with customized solutions for them. Presently, we are operative across five modules of services, as presented below.

Permanent Staffing

Business houses are facing stiff competition in the contemporary business scenario. By hiring productive resources, they can make their profile competitive enough to withstand this challenge. You can opt for our direct placement staffing services while hiring your employees. We provide a comprehensive range of services to our clients, right from identifying, hiring and employing them for permanent positions. The screening process is crucial, and we ensure the highest grades of sincerity and professionalism.

Contract Staffing

At times, you may need qualified staff on short-term basis, working on contracts. Our sound operational mechanism allows us to find the right candidate for our clients. Based on your talent acquisition requirements, we screen the candidates and help you to get the right resources. Most of the IT companies, and other businesses require seasonal workforces. Besides, during the slow periods, you may not need permanent staff, considering the productivity. With our assistance, you can maintain your control over your cost, while retaining talents.

Contract-to-hire Staffing

You can approach us for contract-to-hire staffing services. In this case, you have the option of absorbing an employee under your permanent payroll. Often, clients hire employees on a contractual basis, in order to check out whether they can fulfil their requirements. Based on their project requirements and performance of the employees, they recruit them on a permanent basis. This strategy benefits both the employer and the recruiter. While the employee gets time to get accustomed to the company, it enables the recruiters to evaluate whether the candidate can fulfil the expectations.

Day One Term Hire

Business firms hire key resources to gain a competitive edge. We have this day one term hire option for our clients, where we collaborate them to identify, hire and recruit skilled personnel for critical position. Once you find the candidate you require, you can hire them on a permanent basis. While screening the candidate, we ensure that the person is competent enough for the position. As we operate in close collaboration with our clients, you can have your say over the work proficiency and skills of these candidates.

Project Staffing

Companies requiring specialized staffing solution based on specific projects can approach us for HR management services. This mechanism is appropriate for specific situations like contract or temporary staffing, opening of new branches, seasonal hiring emergent recruitment needs and so on. We have got our own methodology of screening the candidates, based on the individual needs of our clients. With this process-based approach, we carry out scalable programs, ensuring quality services in quick time.

Our Operational Mechanism

A realistic operational mechanism, coupled with timely and quality services defines our profile. We love to work on achievable challenges, strengthening professional bonds with our clients. We follow a scientific and organized operational process to ensure optimum satisfaction to our clients.

  • Evaluating the client's requirements: At the outset, our experts communicate with clients to understand their specific needs.
  • Identifying potential: We round about potential candidates, who can fit the desired profile.
  • Screening the candidate: Through personal interaction, we screen the candidate, making sure that the person can fulfil the job responsibilities.
  • Providing appropriate employees: After screening the candidates, we send their profiles to the client for the final decision-making process.
